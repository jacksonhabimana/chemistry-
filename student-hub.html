How Everything Works

📋 Teacher's Exam (Code)

1

Your teacher creates an exam in the admin portal and gets a 6-digit code.

2

Teacher writes the code on the board or sends it via WhatsApp/email.

3

You enter the code above → the exam loads with the teacher's questions and time limit.

4

Complete the exam → your result goes automatically to the teacher's results dashboard.

⚡ Self-Practice Assessments

1

Quizzes: 30 questions, medium difficulty, immediate feedback and explanations.

2

Tests: 40 harder questions per level, self-marking with detailed explanations.

3

Exams: 50 exam-level questions — most challenging, simulates real exam conditions.

4

All practice questions are unique — different from what's in your course units.
