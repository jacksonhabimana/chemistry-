Welcome to my chemistry learning portal. I'm Jackson Habimana, a chemistry educator based in Kigali, Rwanda, passionate about making chemistry accessible and exciting for every student.

This platform shares notes, lecture slides, practice quizzes, YouTube lessons, exam papers and more with learners at all levels — from secondary school to university.

Chemistry is the language of the universe. My mission is to help students decode it, one molecule at a time — building confidence through clear explanations and great resources.
