S4 Chemistry REB Units & Learning Objectives

Unit Content Learning Objectives
Unit 1 Introduction to Chemistry
  • Understand the importance of chemistry in daily life.
  • Apply laboratory safety rules.
  • Use scientific methods in problem-solving.
Unit 2 Atomic Structure
  • Describe subatomic particles and their properties.
  • Explain isotopes and their applications.
  • Write electron configurations for elements.
Unit 3 Periodic Table
  • Classify elements based on periodic trends.
  • Predict properties using group and period information.
Unit 4 Chemical Bonding
  • Differentiate ionic, covalent, and metallic bonds.
  • Relate bonding to physical properties of substances.
Unit 5 States of Matter
  • Explain kinetic theory of matter.
  • Describe changes of state and energy involved.
Unit 6 Chemical Reactions
  • Identify types of chemical reactions.
  • Balance chemical equations.
  • Apply redox concepts in reactions.
Unit 7 Acids, Bases, and Salts
  • Explain properties of acids and bases.
  • Use the pH scale in analysis.
  • Prepare salts through neutralization.
Unit 8 Stoichiometry
  • Apply the mole concept in calculations.
  • Use Avogadro’s law in problem-solving.
Unit 9 Solutions
  • Define solubility and concentration terms.
  • Prepare solutions of known concentration.
Unit 10 Electrochemistry
  • Explain electrolysis and its applications.
  • Describe electrochemical cells and their uses.
Unit 11 Organic Chemistry (Intro)
  • Identify hydrocarbons and functional groups.
  • Describe simple reactions of alkanes, alkenes, and alcohols.
Unit 12 Environmental Chemistry
  • Discuss sources and effects of pollution.
  • Explain greenhouse gases and climate change.
  • Promote sustainable use of resources.
Unit 13 Practical Work
  • Carry out laboratory experiments safely.
  • Collect and analyze experimental data.
  • Write clear and accurate lab reports.
