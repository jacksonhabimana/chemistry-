S5 Chemistry REB Units & Learning Objectives

Unit Content Learning Objectives
Unit 1 Introduction to Organic Chemistry
  • Understand the scope of organic chemistry.
  • Identify functional groups and nomenclature rules.
Unit 2 Alkanes
  • Explain properties and reactions of alkanes.
  • Apply IUPAC naming conventions.
Unit 3 Alkenes and Alkynes
  • Describe structures and reactivity of unsaturated hydrocarbons.
  • Perform addition reactions and explain mechanisms.
Unit 4 Halogenoalkanes (Alkyl Halides)
  • Explain substitution and elimination reactions.
  • Discuss industrial uses of halogenoalkanes.
Unit 5 Alcohols and Ethers
  • Classify alcohols and ethers.
  • Describe preparation and reactions.
Unit 6 Carbonyl Compounds: Aldehydes and Ketones
  • Identify structures and properties.
  • Explain oxidation and reduction reactions.
Unit 7 Carboxylic Acids and Acyl Chlorides
  • Describe acidity and reactivity.
  • Explain esterification and substitution reactions.
Unit 8 Esters, Acid Anhydrides, Amides and Nitriles
  • Explain synthesis and hydrolysis reactions.
  • Discuss applications in industry.
Unit 9 Amines and Amino Acids
  • Classify amines and amino acids.
  • Explain acid-base behavior and peptide bond formation.
Unit 10 Phase Diagrams
  • Interpret phase diagrams of substances.
  • Explain triple point and critical point.
Unit 11 Solutions and Titration
  • Prepare and analyze solutions.
  • Perform titration calculations accurately.
Unit 12 Conductivity of Solutions
  • Explain ionic conductivity.
  • Relate conductivity to concentration and ion mobility.
Unit 13 Electrolysis
  • Describe electrolysis processes.
  • Apply Faraday’s laws in calculations.
Unit 14 Enthalpy Change of Reactions
  • Define enthalpy and calorimetry.
  • Calculate enthalpy changes using Hess’s law.
Unit 15 Entropy and Free Energy
  • Explain entropy as a measure of disorder.
  • Apply Gibbs free energy in predicting spontaneity.
